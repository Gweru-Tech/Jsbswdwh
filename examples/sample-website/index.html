About Ntando Computer

Ntando Computer was created with a simple mission: to make website deployment accessible to everyone. Whether you're a beginner learning HTML or an experienced developer managing multiple projects, our platform streamlines the deployment process.

We believe that deploying a website should be as simple as sharing a document. No complex configuration, no command-line tools, no server management. Just upload your files and click deploy.
